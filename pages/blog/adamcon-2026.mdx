Can you engineer the perfect day with friends? I'm not sure, but AdamCon is my attempt at making it happen - and after [last year](../adamcon-2025/), it's back for 2026.

AdamCon is a one-day event designed around meeting new people in booked 1:1 conversations. We expect \~30 interesting people from Adam's extended network to attend - find your next best friend, partner, job, cofounder, or just someone to grab a drink with.

We'll be based around the canal and Granary Square at King's Cross - with lots of places to walk and sit if it's sunny, and plenty of interesting indoor space if the weather turns.

## How It Works

- **Fill out your profile** - Name, bio, what you're hoping to get out of AdamCon
- **Browse the directory** - See everyone else's profiles, find people who look interesting
- **Book your meetings** - Reserve 25-minute time slots with other attendees
- **Show up and talk** - No awkward mingling required - just follow your schedule and have real conversations
- **Fill the gaps however you want** - Join impromptu group lunches, hang out by the canal, or book more last-minute meetings

(For people that have used it, this is very similar to [SwapCard](https://www.swapcard.com/features/meeting-request-rules) networking.)

## The Meeting Point

We're based around the canal by Granary Square. To keep things simple, there's just one meeting point: the water fountain (the canalside water refill station). When you book a meeting, that's where you'll find each other. It's a 5-minute walk from King's Cross station.

## Things to do nearby

Got a gap between meetings, or fancy taking a conversation for a wander? King's Cross is a lovely part of town to explore. Some ideas:

- **Walk along the canal**, West towards Gasholder Park and on to Camden Lock or East towards Angel and Islington.
- **Explore Camley Street Natural Park** - a tiny, lovely canalside nature reserve run by the London Wildlife Trust.
- **Grab food at Canopy Market** - a covered market with plenty of food stalls.
- **Head north** to Lewis Cubitt Park, Handyside Gardens or Bingfield Park for some green open space.
- **See Coal Drops Yard** - shops, food and drink in the restored Victorian coal warehouses.
- **Enter Central Saint Martins (UAL)** - the striking Granary building, with its huge covered atrium.
- **Visit St Pancras Library and Leisure Centre** - books, a pool and a gym if you want a change of pace.

## Other questions you might have

**Who's invited?**

AdamCon is open to anyone who wants to meet new people and have interesting conversations. If you know Adam, you should sign up! If you don't know Adam, but you like the sound of this, you should sign up too!

**When do I need to register by?**

Registration closes when we hit capacity. Sign up early to avoid (severe) disappointment.

**How will the matching actually work?**

We built [an app for this](https://github.com/domdomegg/adamcon)! Fill in your profile, browse the directory, and request 25-minute meetings with people you'd like to meet - accepted meetings land on your schedule for the day.

Note the app is just for matching, not applying. To apply, [jump to the registration form at the bottom of this page](#apply) - you'll then get an invite to the app by email after you sign up.

**Do I have to come for the whole day?**

Nope! The event will run from 11am-6pm, but you can drop in for just some of this if you want. Mark your availability in the app, and people will only be able to book time with you when you're actually there.

**What if I don't know anyone?**

AdamCon is designed for people who want to meet new people! You'll be able to find others who are also looking to connect.

**What can I eat/drink?**

AdamCon won't be providing food or drinks, so feel free to bring your own or grab something nearby! There's a ton of great options around Granary Square and Coal Drops Yard.

**How much will it cost?**

It's free! AdamCon is a passion project, not a money-making venture. If you want to support it, just show up and have fun.

**Can I stay overnight?**

If you're travelling a long way for AdamCon, we'll have a few spots at Adam's place for people who want to crash overnight. Let us know in your application.

**I have other decision-relevant questions!**

Contact [Adam](/). Or just sign up and wing it.
